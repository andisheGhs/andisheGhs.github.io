About Me

I am a PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Northeastern University. I am co-advised by Rajmohan Rajaraman and Mahsa Derakhshan.

My research interests lie in theoretical computer science and online algorithms. Currently, my focus is on Correlation Clustering and Online Algorithms with Predictions.

Research Interests

My research focuses on the intersection of theoretical computer science and practical algorithmic challenges. I am particularly interested in:

  • Correlation Clustering: Developing efficient algorithms for clustering problems with pairwise similarity/dissimilarity constraints
  • Online Algorithms with Predictions: Leveraging machine learning predictions to improve online algorithm performance
  • Communication Complexity: Understanding the fundamental limits of distributed computation
  • Natural Sorting Algorithms: Analyzing and optimizing adaptive sorting algorithms
